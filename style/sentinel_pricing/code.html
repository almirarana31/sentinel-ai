Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial compute work? expand_more
Each new account receives 100 free compute units. These can be used for any model training on our shared GPU cluster. Once exhausted, you can upgrade to a paid tier or continue using the limited free community resources.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? expand_more
Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Downgrades will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle, while upgrades are prorated and applied immediately so you can start training faster.
What security measures are in place? expand_more
Sentinel AI uses military-grade encryption for all data at rest and in transit. We are SOC2 Type II compliant and offer VPC isolation for Professional and Enterprise tiers to ensure your intellectual property never leaves your private environment.
Do you offer volume discounts? expand_more
Absolutely. For organizations training hundreds of models monthly, we offer custom throughput-based pricing. Reach out to our sales team for a custom quote based on your specific GPU requirements.
